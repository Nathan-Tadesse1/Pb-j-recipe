PB&J Recipe!

Learn how to make a simple Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Grape-Jelly

    • Instructions

  • Lay out two slices of bread
  • Spread Peanut butter on one slice
  • Spread Jelly on the other slice
  • Put the two slices together
